About

I'm a software developer based in the Western Cape. I work mostly on web applications and small data tools — the kind of projects where careful design up front saves a great deal of debugging later.

This site is where I write about that work. Some posts are walkthroughs of projects I've built; others are notes on the practice of building software, or things I've been reading and thinking about.
